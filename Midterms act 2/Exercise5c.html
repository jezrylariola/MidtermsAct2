Leche Plan

Leche Flan is a dessert made-up of eggs and milk with a soft caramel on top. It resembles crème caramel and caramel custard. This delicious dessert is known throughout the world.It has been a regular item in the menu of most restaurants because of its taste, ease in preparation and long shelf life. It can also be added as a component to build other great tasting dessert creations.

Ingridients:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar.
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 8 eggyolks
  • 2 packs(250ml) All purpose Cream
  • 1 can(400g) Condensed Milk

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180 deg. Celsius.
  2. Make caramel by heating the sugar until it became amber in color with a caramel aroma.
  3. Make the custard by pouring in the egg yolks and whisking them. Pour in the all purpose cream and pour the mixture to the caramel lined pan.
  4. Heat the pan for about 50 minutes.
  5. Refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight.
  6. Serve the pan and get ready to eat it.
